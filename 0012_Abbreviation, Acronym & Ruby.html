Difference between abbr. and acronym

Every acronym is an abbr. but every abbr. is not an acronym.

The only difference is that every abbr. is a standard term whereas acronym lacks such authority.

In HTML5, use of acronym has been abandoned.

Both (semantic in nature) have an attribute 'title'.
