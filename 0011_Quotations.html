Types of Quotations in HTML5

There are two types of quotations (semantic in nature) are in use.

For longer quotations, we use blockquote tag with cite attribute.

For shorter quotations, q tag is used.

Don't use 'q tag' for creating double quotes around a word or phrase.

Blockquote Tag

Anyone who holds a true opinion without understanding is like a blind man on the right road.

Q Tag

As M.Ali Jinnah said, Failure is a word unknown to me.
