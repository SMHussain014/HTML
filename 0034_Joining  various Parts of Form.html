How to take data from outside a form

We can take data even from outside a form, to do this, we have to create an attribute 'id' in form tag.

To call that id, we use an attribute 'form' and assigned it the same title as of id.

We can send our data onto two servers also.

For that we need extra 'action' attribute and 'submit' button.
