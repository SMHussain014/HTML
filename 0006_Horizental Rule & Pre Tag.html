What is White Space

Sometimes, programmers use extra blank space between two words or break a paragraph, just to make them understand the text or code easily. These spaces are called 'White Space'.

Usually, the browsers ignore that White Space and display the text without that white space.

What is a Line Break

How Pre Tag Functions?
