What is White Space

Sometimes, programmers use extra blank space between two words or break a paragraph, just to make them understand the text or code easily. These spaces are called 'White Space'. Like-wise, we have done this by breaking this very paragraph.

Usually, the browsers ignore that White Space and display the text without that white space.

What is a Line Break

We use line break to enhance the beautification of a paragraph.

Line break does not add or remove from the text of a paragraph.

This is an empty element, therefore, needs not to be closed.

For example,
When it rains:
water comes,
if more rain continue, then more water comes.
